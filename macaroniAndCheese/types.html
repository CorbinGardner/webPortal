Types

Baked

Probably the most commone form of macaroni and cheese. Baked in the oven, sometimes broiled for a short time before serving or served with breadcrumbs on top.

Shells

Similar to baked macaroni and cheese, but the pasta is in the form of shells instead of macaroni elbows. Velveeta and American cheese are more commonly seen served with this type of pasta as well.

White Cheddar

A white macaroni and cheese that is most commonly served with a pasta other than the iconic macaroni elbows. Many different types of pasta are served with this less creamy type of cheese that some people prefer.
